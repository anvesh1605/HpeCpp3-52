# AOS-CX and NetEdit Compatibility

This verifies that changes are consistent, compliant, and reduces the amount of errors. Network operators gain the benefits of automated assistance for configuration, with built-in validation and intelligent auditing. Minimal training is required as incumbent teams leverage their existing knowledge and experience.
